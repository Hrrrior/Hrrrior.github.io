    Deity:
    • Mortal Demigods: (about a dozen pop up every year around the globe but most end up getting killed)
      • Mortal with enormous spiritual power. Usually either gain power from people praying to them, performing forgotten rituals or consuming the flesh of a stronger god or demigod. Sometimes cult leaders. Usually reside on earth but can ascend and become immortals by increasing their spiritual power (e.g. eating an entire immortal demigod would probably turn you into one as well).
    • Immortal Demigods: (new one appears once every decade or so)
      • Former mortal Demigods that ascended into the spiritual plane but cannot send spiritual energy to others or interact with higher tier gods. Can return to the physical plane and can also be killed.
    • Fakegod: Main religious gods (true origin forgotten, most gods of major religions have been around for at least a millenium)
      • Former mortal: Immortal demigods whose spiritual power grew exponentially because of a religion based on them. At this point they generally stop thinking and their existence revolves around taking and giving spiritual power. Very difficult to return to a physical body.
      • Wished into existence: Giant mass of spiritual energy that started existing because there were enough people who believed in it. For the most part it would be almost impossible to gain a physical body.
    • Primordial: The first of the mortal and immortal races (believed to be a myth, number unknown)
      • Among the first of all the sentient races. People who reached the spiritual realm while managing to retain sentience and the ability to freely return to the physical plane. Don't need to be believed in to retain their power. Only have some small and obscure religions dedicated to them.

    Devils:
    • Demidevils: (Hide among the general populus. Thought to be just some type of monster. Atempt to charm, scam or swindle people into signing over their soul.)
      • Former mortal Demigods that descended into the infernal plane. Due to risk of turning into a demon if their spiritual power should decline to a certain point, they constantly try to trick mortals into giving away their souls. Can return to the physical plane and can also be killed.
    • Devils: (Appear before people in despair to try and trick them into signing their souls. Most religions see them as the direct enemies of their god.)
      • Former Demidevils: No longer at risk of turning into a demon, they still constantly try to trick mortals into giving away their souls. By gaining power and retaining their mortal desires, their motives and ambitions become increasingly more evil. Can return to the physical plane and can also be killed.
      • Feared into existence: Either from peoples fear and despair or from a bloodbath with many dead devils. Somewhere in the infernal plane a mass of energy pops into existence. Despite never having been a mortal, they acquire some of their most twisted desires. Can return to the physical plane, but difficult to kill.
    • Archdevils: (From a mortal standpoint not overly distinguishable from devils. Sometimes force weak willed people to do things without having to appear in front of them.)
      • Former Devils: Rulers of the infernal realm. No longer waste time on getting mortal souls. If necessary they consume the souls of other devils or even gods. Occasionally desire to cause mischief in the physical plane by putting thoughts into peoples heads. Hard to return to the physical realm. Can only be killed by a Fakegod, Primordial, Great One or the combined power of 66 devils.
