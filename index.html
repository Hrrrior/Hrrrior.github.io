    History/Major Historic events:

    • Current year: The third era - 500 After the Great Meteor Shower (AMS500)
    • (AMS0) 500 years ago a meteor shower hit the better half of the civilized world. Much of history and some of the greatest buildings, forests and monunuments that ever existed, were wiped from the earth in an instant.
    • (AMS332) Despite not having fully recovered from the event, many explorers went out into the world to discover places that had not been destoyed by the tragedy
    • (AMS334) Monster appearances had reached an all time high in 100 years. Hutings monsters, beasts and other abberations became a well respected job.
    • (AMS335) Explorers discovered a brand new continent. Made contact with the cat folk inhabitants.
    • (AMS337) A human empire attempts to conquer the new world.
    • (AMS339) Despite having a bigger army and more advanced technology, the humans lose and are forced to surrender.
    • (AMS345) The cat tribes form an empire and are recognized as an independant nation.
    • (AMS395 - AMS399) A great plague known as the "Arcane Pain"(WIP) sweeps the world and erradicates 93% percent of all (pure) magic users.
    • (AMS407) With the ever growing monster threat, 5 of the most well known and strongest Hunter families/clans band together to create a Global Organization to battle evil.
    • (AMS460) Technology makes great advances. Organization cuts down threats in all over world.
    • (AMS465) With declining monster number also the need for hunters becomes lower. The power of the steam engine is a scientifc revolution.
    • (AMS476) A conflic arises between two neighboring nations and a domino effect causes the entire world to become involved. This later becomes known as the Global War.
    • (AMS477) Many Hunters leave the Organization to go fight for their countries. The Organization HQ is located in a neutral zone however the war makes traveling the world difficult, but monster sightings do no rise as a result.
    • (AMS483) The Global War finally ends. Much of the world has once more been destroyed.
    • (AMS490) 7 years after the Global War ended. The light bulb is invented.
    • (AMS500) For 3 years monster sigtings have been rapitly increasing again and the Organization is looking for new recruits.
